Rules

Basic Rules

You share your Amazon.com Wish List with {{ PROJECT_TITLE }}. {{ PROJECT_TITLE }} attempts to extract all items from it. You pick one item from the list and that gives you a unique page just for you for that item.

Before your page is ready to be shared with the world you need to verify that the email address you set it up with really is yours. All you need to do is click a link in an email sent to you.

You are then responsible for sharing this page with your friends and family. Hopefully your friends and family make contributions towards the total price of the item you chose.

As soon as the first contribution is made, the clock starts ticking. If the total price of the item is not reached within 30 days all contributions made so far are cancelled and contributors are fully refunded.

When the full price of the item is reached, {{ PROJECT_TITLE }} will contact you and confirm your address and then simply buy the item from your Amazon Wish List.

Fine Print Rules

Items you choose must be over $10.00.

{{ PROJECT_TITLE }} reserves the right to cancel any Wish Lists created without prior notice. If doing so, all pending contributions will be fully refunded.

Only Wish Lists whose address is in the United States are allowed. Addresses outside the United States may either be cancelled or you might need to pay additionally for the shipping.
