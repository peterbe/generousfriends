Help & Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any questions about how to use the site or any other suggestions for improvements don't hesitate to send an email to mail@wishlistgranted.com

What happens after 30 days is up and the total price is not reached?

If only a relatively small amount is remaining, you can make a contribution of your own.
If the full amount is not reached within 30 days, all contributors will have their payments refunded at no additional expense.

Can I contribute to my own Wish List?

Yes you certainly can. Perhaps your friends have contributed some but not all, you can contribute the remainder.

If I make a contribution and the total is not reached, do I get my money back?

Yes! If the total of the desired item on a Wish List is NOT reached within 30 days from the first contribution made, all contributions made are refunded.

Is this site secure enough to protect my credit card?

Yes! Because we never store any credit card details at all. It goes between you and Balanced Payments™ over a secure connection.

Why can't I just give you my email address I use on Amazon.com?

It's actually impossible. Because of privacy. The instructions might be complicated but this way your privacy is guraranteed.
