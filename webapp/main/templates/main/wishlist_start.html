How-to on emailing it in

The following step-by-step instructions are for setting up your Wish List on a iPhone by email. It will look different but should work very similarly on Android and any other smart phones.

1. Go to www.amazon.com

You might need to sign in first to see your Wish List

2. Click the "Share List" button

Your list must be made public. If it's not you can't share it and there should be instructions there to change your list to being public.

3. Click to share by "Email"

4. Type in mail@inbound.wishlistgranted.com

After you have typed in the email address, note that you might need to scroll down to see the "Share via email" button.

5. Check your email, click verification link

It may take a couple of minutes to receive an email about the verification link. It is sent to email address you're signed in to Amazon.com with.

6. Pick your most preferred item on your Wish List

Hopefully all items from your Wish List are there.

7. Your Wish List is ready to be shared with friends and family
